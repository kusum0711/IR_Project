Cimahi (Indonesian pronunciation: [t Éimahi]) is a city located immediately west of the larger city of Bandung, in West Java Province, Indonesia and within the Bandung Metropolitan Area. It covers an area of 40.37 km2 and had a population at the 2010 Census of 541,177 and at the 2020 Census of 568.400. The city is a major textile producer, and is home to several military training centres.
