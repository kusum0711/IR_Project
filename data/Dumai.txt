Dumai (Jawi: دوماي‎, Chinese: 杜迈), is a city in Riau province on the island of Sumatra, Indonesia. The city has an area of 1,727.38 km² and had 253,803 inhabitants at the 2010 Census, 285,448 at the 2015 Census and 316,782 at the 2020 Census. Dumai has a domestic airport, Pinang Kampai Airport. Dumai is an important transport and trade centre, both regionally and internationally, especially to Malaysia.
Dumai is rich in oil (petroleum and palm oil).
