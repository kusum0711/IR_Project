Bhilai is a city in the Indian state of Chhattisgarh, in eastern central India. With a population of 1,064,077, Bhilai is the second-largest urban area in Chhattisgarh after Raipur. Bhilai is a major industrial city in India as well as an education hub of central India. The Bhilai metropolis contains three municipal corporations: Bhilai Municipal Corporation, Bhilai-Charoda Municipal Corporation and Risali Municipal Corporation.
